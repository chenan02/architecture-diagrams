Floor Insulation Information

Several types of floor insulation are compared in a benchmark analysis

These Uvalues are predefined for comparison:

  • U-value 0.1 W/m2K (super high performance building)
  • U-value 0.15 W/m2K (high performance building)
  • U-value 0.18 W/m2K (good performance building)
  • U-value 0.2 W/m2K (good standard)
  • U-value 0.25 W/m2K (good standard)
  • U-value 0.3 W/m2K (medium)
  • U-value 0.5 W/m2K (low)
  • U-value 0.8 W/m2K (very low)

NOTE: Comments are for buildings in cold climates
