Geometry Information

Volume:

Volume of conditioned space in m3. The volume includes the construction (walls) of the building

Area:

Gross floor area of the conditioned space in m2

Number of floors:

The total number of floors of the conditioned space

Building height:

The height of the building (conditioned space only). The number is needed for the estimation of the energy demand for ventilation

Wall area:

The total area of the external wall faced to the given orientation (in m2). Area includes the window area

Window to wall ratio:

The percentage of the window area in the wall area. 100% means that the wall is completely glazed

Window area:

Area of the window in the wall faced to a given orientation. The area is calculated automatically by the tool

Roof area:

Area of the roof in m2

Floor area:

Area of the floor in m2

Floor adjacent:

User can define the adjacency of the floor

  • “To ground” means that the floor is adjacent to the ground. The tool calculates the ground temperature at a depth of 2 meters
  • “To Unheated space” means that the floor is adjacent to an unconditioned space like an attic. A reduction factor is taken into account to reduce the temperature difference between the internal and external
  • ”To external” means the floor is exposed to the external

Roof adjacency:

User can define the adjacency of the roof

  • ”To external” means the roof is exposed to the external
  • “To Unheated space” means that the floor is adjacent to an unconditioned space like an attic. A reduction factor is taken into account to reduce the temperature difference between the internal and external
