Construction Information

A benchmark study for the parameter construction is given for four predefined cases. The building construction is classified in four parameter settings. The thermal capacity of the construction is defined for the ceiling and refers to the ceiling area

Construction Types:

Light :

  • Light building construction without internal thermal mass
  • Typical example of a timber construction

Medium :

  • Building with a medium internal thermal mass
  • Typical example of a building with uncovered concrete ceiling and light dividing walls

Heavy I :

  • Building with a heavy construction and a lot of internal thermal mass
  • Typical example of a building with uncovered concrete ceiling and heavy dividing walls

Heavy II :

  • Building with a very heavy construction and a large amount of internal thermal mass
  • Typical example of a building with uncovered concrete ceiling and heavy dividing walls and uncovered heavy floor

The diagram on the left side shows the room temperature and the external temperature of the building. The temperature values are average values for each day in the year. The room temperature does not include active heating and cooling. The user can see the temperature of the other parameter settings by clicking on the setting icon

NOTE: The diagram on the right side shows the annual energy demand value for heating and cooling in kWh/m2year
