Night Ventilation Information

The comparison shows the building equipped without and with night ventilation. Here, natural ventilation for night ventilation is defined by ventilation due to operable windows. The air change rate of the natural ventilation is fixed with ACH 1.5 and ACH 3

ACH 1.5 [1/h]: estimation for single sided ventilation

ACH 3 [1/h]: estimation for cross ventilation

The night ventilation is activated as long the internal temperature and humidity is in the comfort field

NOTE: The diagram on the left side shows the room temperature and the external temperature of the building. The temperature values are average values for each day in the year. The room temperature does not include active heating and cooling. The user can see the temperature of the other parameter settings by clicking on the setting icon

NOTE: The diagram on the right side shows the annual energy demand value for heating and cooling in kWh/m2year
