Shading Information

Several predefined shading systems are compared in a benchmark study. The tool calculated the shading effect dynamically in each hour of the year

Overhang 30°:

The overhang has a shading angle of 30°

Overhang and Wingwall:

The building is equipped with an overhang and wingwall combination. Shading angle of the Overhang and wingwall is 30°

Vertical Brise Soleil:

The building is equipped with a vertical fixed shading system. The shading elements are oriented with its open site to the south to receive sun radiation in the winter and to reduce sun radiation in the summer

Horizontal Lamella:

A horizontal lamella structure is mounted as an external shading device. Shading angle is 25°

Movable Shading:

Movable shading system controlled for radiation on façade and room temperature. FC value of shading system 0.12 [-]

Horizontal Lamella Controlled:

Same system like the Horizontal Lamella. Lamellas are rotatable to be adjustable to the solar radiation

Fixed, predefined Shading coefficient 0.8 (80% transmittance)

Fixed, predefined Shading coefficient 0.5 (50% transmittance)

Fixed, predefined Shading coefficient 0.3 (30% transmittance)
