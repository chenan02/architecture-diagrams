Infiltration Information

Different qualities of the building air tightness are compared in a benchmark study. The air tightness (infiltration) includes joints of the window frame and other not wanted air leaks of the facade. Ventilation caused by operable windows is not included in this analysis (interface Natural Ventilation)

ACH = Air change rate [1/h]

Ratio infiltrated air to air volume of building. Infiltrated air in one hour

ACH < 0.15 [1/h]:

Very air tight building like the European passive house. Proper construction details to reduce air infiltration are needed

ACH 0.15-0.25 [1/h]:

Average air tight building of new building in cold climate without any consideration of building details

ACH > 0.25 [1/h]:

Building not designed to reduce air infiltration like old (not renovated) buildings
