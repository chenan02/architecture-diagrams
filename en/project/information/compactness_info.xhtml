Compactness Information

Recommendations for the compactness of the building are given

  • The dimensionless envelope to area ratio in the diagram is a measure for the compactness
  • The envelope includes all surfaces facing to the external or to an unheated adjacent space (walls, floor and roof)
  • The area describes the gross floor area of all floors
  • The diagram on the left side shows the room temperature and the external temperature of the building
  • The temperature values are average values for each day in the year
  • The room temperature does not include active heating and cooling
  • The user can see the temperature of the other parameter settings by clicking on the setting icon

NOTE: The diagram on the right side shows the annual energy demand value for heating and cooling in kWh/m2year
