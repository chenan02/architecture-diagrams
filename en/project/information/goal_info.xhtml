Goal Information

An Energy goal can be defined for the project. The tool will calculate an optimal strategy to meet the goal. The goal is the sum of the energy demand for sensible heating, sensible cooling, humidification and dehumidification. The goal for the energy demand can be defined by the user. Recommendations will be done comparing calculation results to this value

Typical values:

Minimal energy demand given by national standards

Low energy:

40% less than national standard goal

Passive house:

15 kWh/m2 year (for middle Europe)

NOTE: The energy goal is evaluated by calculating the sum of the energy demand for heating, cooling, humidification and dehumidification

Maximal temperature:

The maximal acceptable room temperature in oC. Room will be cooled if value is exceeded. This temperature is like the set point temperature at a thermostat

Minimal temperature:

The minimal acceptable room temperature in oC. Room will be heated if value is exceeded. This temperature is like the set point temperature at a thermostat

Maximal relative humidity:

Maximal acceptable relative humidity in the room. Room will be dehumidified if value is exceeded

Minimal relative humidity:

Minimal acceptable relative humidity in the room. Room will be humidified if value is exceeded

Heating energy consideration:

Software recommendations will consider the heating energy demand, when drop down is set to “on”

Cooling energy consideration:

Software recommendations will consider the cooling energy demand, when drop down is set to “on”
