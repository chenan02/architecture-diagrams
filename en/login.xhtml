Comfort and Climate is a simulation tool for determining the energy demand of a building. It helps you specify your design decisions in a user-friendly way. Type in the information that is already available about your future building. The web application will make suggestions about the missing parts and indicate how you can improve your choices. The dynamic simulation of the energy demand for attaining the inside comfort is updated at each step. See Why Comfort and Climate?
